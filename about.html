About HuttDrive Motors

We are a trusted car dealership in Lower Hutt, specializing in Mazda vehicles. Our mission is to provide exceptional cars with excellent customer service, backed by years of experience in the automotive industry.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, HuttDrive Motors has been serving the Lower Hutt area for over a decade. Our passion for cars and commitment to customer satisfaction has made us a leading name in the automotive industry.

Our Mission

At HuttDrive Motors, our mission is to offer a wide range of high-quality vehicles, with a focus on Mazda cars. We aim to make the car buying process easy and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're a first-time buyer or looking for an upgrade, we have something for you!

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Knowledge: Our team of professionals has years of experience in the automotive industry, ensuring you get the best advice and service.
  • Quality Assurance: All of our vehicles undergo a thorough inspection to guarantee they meet the highest standards.
  • Customer-Focused: We are committed to providing a stress-free and transparent car buying experience. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
  • Local Business: As a locally-owned business, we care deeply about the community and are dedicated to giving back.
